How to interpret the results?
  • Risk: A rating associated with the site, anything above 4 should be considered suspicious.
  • First Seen: The date when the site was first observed by Netcraft. Be aware that sites with an 'undefined' date (new sites) may not have an accurate rating.
  • Pattern Matches: The number of threat patterns that match the given input. Even a single match indicates confirmed malicious activity.
  • Note on Netblock / IP: The IP address and netblock may not match when CDNs are involved.
